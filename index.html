About

About Arnie & It's creator

Hi, I'm Mat! I'm a 26 year old software developer and regular gym-goer from Reading in the UK. Arnie is currently a personal side project that I develop in my spare time. I had wanted to experiment with chatbots for a while and thought a bot that could help me remember my gym workouts and log progress would be the perfect candidate. Subscribe to stay updated!
